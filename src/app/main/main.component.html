Sign-ups

Basic Information

  1. Maximum of 2800 to be made.
  2. Must be a resident of Schenectady County.
  3. Must have one of the qualifying IDs to sign up.
  4. One basket per household.
  5. Baskets can only be picked up in a vehicle. No "walkers" this year.

Qualifying ID:

  • HEAP Award Letter
  • Referral from Community Agency or Community Leader
  • Benefit Card
  • SSI Award Letter
  • WIC Card
  • Active, current unemployment Information
  • Proof of Residency in Municipal Housing
  • SSD Award Letter

We DO NOT Accept Social Security Cards, Social Security Award Letter or Medicare Cards.
