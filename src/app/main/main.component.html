Sign-ups for the 2020 Thanksgiving Food Drive

Basic Information

  1. Maximum of 2800 baskets will be made.
  2. Must be a resident of Schenectady County.
  3. Must have one of the qualifying ID's listed below to sign up.
  4. Limit of one basket per household.
  5. Baskets must be picked up in a vehicle. No "walkers" this year.

Qualifying ID:

  • HEAP Award Letter (Home Energy Assistance Program)
  • Referral from Community Agency or Community Leader
  • Benefit Card
  • SSI Award Letter (Supplemental Security Income)
  • WIC Card (Supplemental Nutrition Program for Women, Infants, and Children)
  • Active, current unemployment Information
  • Proof of Residency in Municipal Housing
  • SSD Award Letter (Social Security Disability)

We DO NOT Accept Social Security Cards, Social Security Award Letter or Medicare Cards.
